Privacy Policy

FinEvo is an offline expense and budget tracking application. Your privacy is important to us.

Information Collection and Use

FinEvo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data to our servers. All expense and budget information entered by users remains locally on the device.

Third-Party Advertising

FinEvo uses third-party advertising services, specifically Google AdMob, to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Data collected by AdMob may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Interaction with advertisements

This data is handled in accordance with Google’s privacy policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

All expense and budget data is stored locally on the user’s device only. FinEvo does not back up or sync data to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

FinEvo does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
